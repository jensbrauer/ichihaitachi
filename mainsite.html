Background

Uchiha Itachi is a fictional character who appeared in the popular Manga and Anime series Naruto and Naruto Shippuden.
The iconic character poses a moral dilemma for viewers as he is guilty of slaughtering his entire clan, only with the exception of his younger brother Sasuke.

Naruto Anime

In the very popular Naruto and Naruto Shippuden anime, Itachi is the older brother of main character Sasuke.

To get an understanding of the context, watch this fan made trailer.

ITACHIS STORY

In the series fictional timeline, powerful ninja clans and villages compete for power.

Itachi grows up as a member of the renowned hidden leaf villages most prominent clan "The Uchiha".

ITACHIS MOTIVES

Itachi shows his memories to his younger brother, in order to explain his actions.

Until this moment in the series, Itachi is portrayed as a brutal murderer and villain.

A popular youtube meme is to record the reactions to this revealing as it is often deeply emotional for the fans.

HERO / VILLAIN ?

Despite revealing his good intentions to the viewers, Itachi is still judged harshly by many.

Was he a dark knight bearing a painful burden for the sake of peace, or a selfish rogue only interested in himself and his younger brother?
