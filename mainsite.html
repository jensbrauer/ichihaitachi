Background

Uchiha Itachi is a fictional character who appeared in the popular Manga and Anime series Naruto and Naruto Shippuden.
The iconic character poses a moral dilemma for viewers as he is guilty of slaughtering his entire clan, only with the exception of his younger brother Sasuke.
The reasons for his actions are of immense importance for the plot of the series and both get to experience him as ultimate evil as well as a hero cast into darkness for the sake of the greater good.
